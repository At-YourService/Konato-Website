Working @ Konato

Konato has the ambition to grow and is looking for new colleagues. We aspire self-development and entrepreneurship, while maintaining an open culture at the core of our company. We screen on personality, talent and expertise. Our company’s DNA is built on integrity, trust and respect. And a can-do mentality! We believe in “hiring for attitude and training for skill”.

Life @ Konato

We believe that your knowledge and contributions are the strength of our future! Therefor we invest in our employees. Training & coaching is at the heart of our organization. We have a culture that supports employee growth and an environment that subscribes to success through teamwork. We organize knowledge sharing events to increase your expertise. Beside the professional events, we try to have as much fun as possible with our Family Days, Sinterklaas parties, team weekends, etc.
